About

The main inspiration behind the idea of this project was a visit I made to a tourist center in Lagos some time ago. There was a treehouse, and inside the treehouse, a section of it was filled with signatures and short notes made by people that had been there in the past.
Reading texts left behind by strangers from different part of the world left behind a sense of a community that I wanted to portray in this web application, and that inspired the creation of a web page that people could leave behind messages on.
